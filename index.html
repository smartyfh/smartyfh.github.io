Welcome! My name is Fanghua Ye. I am a second-year PhD student at UCL, working with Prof. Emine Yilmaz. Prior to that, I received my master's degree from Sun Yat-sen University and my bachelor's degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), where I studied in Yingcai Honors College of UESTC.

My current research is focused on conversational AI. I also have a strong passion in graph learning.
