Welcome to my homepage! My name is Fanghua Ye. I have been a master student at the School of Data and Computer Science of Sun Yat-sen University since 2016. I am working in the InPlus Lab, under the supervision of Prof. Zibin Zheng. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), where I studied in Yingcai Honors College of UESTC (Elite school of top 5% undergraduates).

My current research interests include network science, algorithm design, machine learning, and data mining. I have a strong passion in developing machine learning algorithms for mining deep knowledge from graph/relational structured data.
